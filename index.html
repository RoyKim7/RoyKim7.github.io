Interests

When I was in elementary school, I had chance to visit the United States since my father went to US as an Industrial Engineering visiting professor. As I visited a myriad of places in US with my parents, there were so many rich people and big buildings in certain places of the US whereas there were many others in other parts who were suffering pain and needed help. Especially, I could see a lot of people who did not had place to stay or food to eat. Even though I was very young at that time, these scenes left a big impression on me, and I promised myself to be a person that can give help to those people in the future.
